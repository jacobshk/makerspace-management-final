What is a makerspace?

Libraries and schools often have public "makerspaces" These are rooms that contain machines, such as 3D printers or laser engravers, freely available for public use.

What does Makerspace Management do?

Makerspace Management is a free to use website designed to aid in management of makerspaces. Users can use MM for all of their makerspace's logistical needs, from the proprietary reservation system, to the user management system, and beyond!
